# SURVIVOR.IO-Endless-Wave-Shooter
🎮 SURVIVOR.IO is a top-down survival shooter!  You are alone against endless waves of alien enemies. Move, dodge, and let your weapons fire automatically. Your only job: SURVIVE as long as possible!  ⚔️ FEATURES - Endless waves — gets harder every round - Level Up system — choose 1 of 3 upgrades - 10 unique upgrades to combine - 4 enemy types
